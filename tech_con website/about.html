Here is a detailed history of TechCon

In the year 2019, Adam Lwanga, the CEO to All Things Tech Comapny established the first ever Tech Conference in Nairobi. His aim was to recruit both young and talented youths in various tech fields.During that time Adam managed to empower and recruit many young unemployed young youths and in the year 2021 after the pandemic,Adam hosted the second TechCon summit in Mombasa which saw many attendees especially the youths from different countries.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to advancetechnology among the youth and empowering them.
